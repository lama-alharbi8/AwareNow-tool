## üìå Project Description
AwareNow is a web-based cybersecurity awareness platform designed to help organizations improve their employees‚Äô awareness of cybersecurity threats such as phishing attacks, weak passwords, and unsafe digital behavior.

The platform provides structured and centralized cybersecurity awareness solutions, including training courses, quizzes, phishing simulation campaigns, awareness email campaigns, password security tools, and security policy management.

AwareNow is offered as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solution for organizations, enabling them to manage employees, monitor awareness levels, and ensure compliance with cybersecurity policies. The platform is localized to meet the needs of organizations operating in Saudi Arabia.

---

## ‚ùó Problem Statement
Many organizations face increasing cybersecurity risks due to low employee awareness. Human error remains one of the leading causes of phishing attacks, data breaches, and unsafe digital practices.

Organizations often lack:
- Localized cybersecurity awareness content.
- Effective tools to measure employee awareness and compliance.
- Practical methods to test real employee behavior, such as phishing simulations.
- Centralized platforms to manage awareness programs and security policies.

---

## ‚úÖ Proposed Solution
AwareNow addresses these challenges by providing:
- A centralized cybersecurity awareness platform for organizations.
- Localized content tailored to Saudi organizations.
- Phishing simulation campaigns to evaluate real employee behavior.
- Awareness email campaigns to reinforce best security practices.
- Monitoring and reporting tools to measure awareness and compliance.
- Scalable subscription plans based on organization size.
